Who Am I?

Hello! Welcome to my personal portfolio. My name is Subin Kim and I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of California, Irvine. Currently, I am pursuing a bachelors degree in Computer Science.

I was born in California but traveled a lot early on in my life for my father's work. I have lived in China for two years and have traveled all around the West Coast of the U.S. I love to travel and see all the different types of cultures I have never been exposed to.

In my freetime I love to play basketball with friends, read the lastest realistic fictions, or go on a run around the park. These interests are what help me stay positive and feel rejuvenated whenever I need that boost. I also love getting involved with my community and try to help out by volunteering whenever possible.

I developed an interest in computer science when I started an intro course to computer science while in high school. This wasn't any intro course to CS, this was my high school's first-ever computer science course ever introduced to the school. Knowing this, it inspired me to learn more about a subject that I and fellow peers had never been accustomed to.

I hope that my passion for computer science and ambition for learning help me continue to develop as a better individual.

Users can also select through multiple learning modes, one of those modes being a pronunciation practice. Coded using P5 JS, P5 Speech API, and Teachable Machine API.
